More Than a Century of Passion.

We didn't just witness the evolution of the automobile. We drove it.

Our Legacy is Our Launchpad

It started in 1916. While our first products conquered the skies as aircraft engines, our true destiny was on the road. From the first motorcycle to the iconic kidney grille, our story has been one of relentless innovation.

Today, Bayerische Motoren Werke AG is a symbol, not just of luxury, but of what happens when you refuse to compromise on performance. We don't just build cars. We craft experiences. We engineer joy.

This showcase is a tribute to that legacy—a line in the sand that stretches from our past, through our present, and into the electric future we're building *today*.

A Timeline of Innovation

1916

Our Founding

Bayerische Flugzeugwerke AG (BFW) is born. A year later, it becomes BMW. Our journey from the clouds to the road begins.

1972

The M-Series

BMW Motorsport GmbH is founded. The "world's fastest letter" is born, and the racetrack is forever changed. This is our performance soul.

2013

The 'i' Revolution

The all-electric BMW i3 is launched. We prove that the "Ultimate Driving Machine" doesn't need gasoline to have a heartbeat.

"The ultimate driving machine isn't built — it's born from a relentless, uncompromising passion for the road."
